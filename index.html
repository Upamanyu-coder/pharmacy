About Us

Prathana Pharmacy is a trusted healthcare destination located in Butwal, opposite the Lumbini Provincial Hospital. We are dedicated to serving our community with professional pharmaceutical services and expert healthcare advice.

Our pharmacy is staffed by licensed and experienced pharmacists who are committed to ensuring the health and well-being of every patient who walks through our doors.

Important Notice

We do not sell medicines online. Please visit our physical location for all your pharmaceutical needs. Our licensed pharmacists are available to provide personalized consultation and ensure safe medication dispensing.
